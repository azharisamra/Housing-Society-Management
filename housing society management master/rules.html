Rules & Regulations

It is mandatory for all the members of the society to maintaing the rules also for the committee members to ensure that the society rules are being followed.

GENERAL RULES AND REGULATIONS APPLICABLE TO FLAT OWNER/TENANTS


1. Each owner/tenant shall abide by Rules & Regulation related to membership / flats / apartments allotted under the group housing scheme of HUDA & HEWO
apartments association sector 15 part-II Gurgaon & also to abide by other rules / regulations /direction of the society as enforced from time to time.

2. Upon any apartment owner selling his flat the purchaser shall be admitted as member on payment of entrance fee. The share held by the flat owner shall
be transferred to the name of the purchaser.

3. Each owner / tenant shall pay the maintenance charger demanded by society monthly or otherwise and to pay the same even if the flat is not occupied for
the part of the period.

4. Each owner shall not make any alteration,addition, sub-division,extension,amalgamation or carry out any structural design or layout change to the flat
without the approval of Managing committee of HEWO apartment association Gurgaon in writing and further subject to the prior approval of building by HUDA.

5. Each owner shall be responsible for the internal repairs of the flat allotted to him and shall not at any time cause any damage to the building or the
estate or disrupt common services of the society.

6. Each owner / tenant shall not do anything which in the opinion of society may cause a nuisance, annoyance or inconvenience to the occupants adjacent or
in the neighborhood or be the prejudicial to the society.

7. Flat would be used for the sole purpose of living and the space meant for car/vehicle parking on the ground floor would be used for parking of vehicles
only and not for any commercial use.

8. The facilities like staircases,passages, terraces, parks, lift and common space and services will be utilized with other beneficiaries and will not have
exclusive right of usage not making alteration thereto.

9. NEW TENANTS will not be allowed to enter HEWO compound with household articles unit and unless the flat owner has deposited all outstanding dues of
association.

10. An owner / tenant shall reimburse the association for any expenditure incurred in repairing or replacing any common area and facility damaged through
his/her fault.

11. For other rules and regulation contact Manager, HEWO office FLAT No. 98 ( Contact here).
